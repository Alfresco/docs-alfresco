``pad(string, length)``
-   **[getNodeFromString](../references/API-JS-getNodeFromString.md)**  
``getNodeFromString(noderef)``

``longQName(string)``
-   **[createQName](../references/API-JS-createQName.md)**  
 ``createQName(string)``
-   **[moduleInstalled](../references/API-JS-moduleInstalled.md)**  
 ``moduleInstalled(string)``
