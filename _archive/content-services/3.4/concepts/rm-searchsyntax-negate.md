# Search for negation

Single terms, phrases, and so on can be combined using "NOT" in upper, lower, or mixed case, or prefixed with "!" or "-".

```
yellow NOT banana
yellow !banana
yellow -banana
NOT yellow banana
-yellow banana
!yellow banana
```
